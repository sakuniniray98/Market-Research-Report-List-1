<p><strong>The Large Silicon Wafers For Integrated Circuits Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Czochralski Method</li><li>Float Zone Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The large silicon wafers market for integrated circuits features primarily two growth methods: the Czochralski (CZ) method and the Float Zone (FZ) method. The Czochralski method involves melting high-purity silicon and pulling a single crystal from the melt, producing larger, cost-effective wafers suitable for mass production. In contrast, the Float Zone method utilizes high-purity silicon rods and a localized melting technique to achieve even higher purity, making it ideal for advanced semiconductor applications where performance is critical.</p></p>
