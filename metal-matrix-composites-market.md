<p><strong>The Metal-matrix Composites Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder Metallurgy</li><li>Liquid-permeable</li><li>Foundry</li><li>Deposition Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal-matrix composites (MMCs) are categorized based on their production techniques. Powder metallurgy involves compacting metal powders and sintering them to form dense materials. Liquid-permeable methods embed reinforcing materials into molten metal, enhancing properties during solidification. Foundry techniques utilize traditional casting mixed with reinforcements to create customized composites. Deposition technology includes processes like thermal spray or chemical vapor deposition, allowing for controlled layering of materials, which results in tailored physical characteristics and improved performance in various applications.</p></p>
