<p><strong>The Composite Material in Dental Restoration Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Resin-based Composites</li><li>Poly-acid Modified Composites</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Composite materials in dental restoration primarily include resin-based composites and poly-acid modified composites. Resin-based composites are widely utilized due to their aesthetic appeal, strength, and versatility in restoring teeth. They bond effectively to the tooth structure, providing durability. Poly-acid modified composites, also known as glass ionomer composites, release fluoride and offer a chemical bond to the tooth, enhancing decay resistance. These materials cater to varying clinical needs, balancing appearance, strength, and longevity in dental repairs.</p></p>
