<p><ul><li>Serum Sample</li><li>Plasma Sample</li><li>Whole Blood Sample</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiovascular disease test kit market comprises three primary types based on sample types: serum samples, plasma samples, and whole blood samples. Serum samples, obtained after blood clotting, are used for various biochemical analyses and can provide specific insights into heart health. Plasma samples, which retain blood components and are anticoagulated, are valuable for testing various biomarkers. Whole blood samples, collected directly without separation, offer rapid testing and are advantageous for point-of-care applications, enhancing accessibility to cardiovascular diagnostics.</p></p>
