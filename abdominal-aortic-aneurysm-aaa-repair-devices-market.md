<p><p>The Abdominal Aortic Aneurysm (AAA) Repair Devices market primarily includes synthetic graft systems and endovascular stent graft systems. Synthetic graft systems involve the surgical placement of a durable, woven fabric tube to reinforce the aorta and prevent rupture. In contrast, endovascular stent graft systems are minimally invasive devices inserted through the femoral artery, placing a stent within the aneurysm to support the vessel wall. Both types aim to enhance patient outcomes by effectively managing AAA while minimizing procedural risks.</p></p>
