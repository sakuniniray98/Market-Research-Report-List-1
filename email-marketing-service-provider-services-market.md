<p><p>Email Marketing Service Providers (ESPs) can be categorized into two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise ESPs require businesses to install and manage email marketing software on their own servers, providing greater control and customization. Conversely, cloud-based ESPs host the software on shared servers, offering scalability and ease of use, as well as automatic updates and maintenance. Both options cater to different business needs, considering factors like budget, technical expertise, and the scale of email campaigns.</p></p>
