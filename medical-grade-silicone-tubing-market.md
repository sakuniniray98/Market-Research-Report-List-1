<p><strong>The Medical Grade Silicone Tubing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Platinum Vulcanization</li><li>Peroxide Vulcanization</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical-grade silicone tubing market is primarily categorized into two types: platinum vulcanization and peroxide vulcanization. Platinum vulcanization involves a catalyst that ensures a clean, high-quality product with excellent biocompatibility and resistance to aging and heat, making it ideal for sensitive medical applications. In contrast, peroxide vulcanization uses a different curing agent that can result in a cost-effective solution, though it may offer lower biocompatibility and less flexible properties. Both types serve distinct purposes based on the specific requirements of medical devices.</p></p>
