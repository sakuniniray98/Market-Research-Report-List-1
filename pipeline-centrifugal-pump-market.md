<p><p>The pipeline centrifugal pump market is categorized into three types based on operating pressure: low pressure, medium pressure, and high pressure. Low pressure centrifugal pumps are designed for applications with less than 1,000 psi, ideal for water transport and irrigation. Medium pressure pumps range from 1,000 to 1,500 psi, suitable for industrial processes requiring moderate pressure. High pressure centrifugal pumps operate above 1,500 psi, commonly used in oil and gas, chemical processing, and other demanding applications requiring high flow and pressure.</p></p>
