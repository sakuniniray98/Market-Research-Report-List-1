<p><p>Endoscopic reprocessors are essential for the safe cleaning and sterilization of endoscopes. The market is divided into two main types: single basin and dual basin reprocessors. Single basin units offer a compact design, ideal for facilities with limited space, allowing for effective cleaning and disinfection in one chamber. Dual basin reprocessors provide a more efficient workflow, utilizing separate chambers for cleaning and disinfecting, which enhances performance and reduces cross-contamination risks. Both types cater to different facility needs and operational capacities.</p></p>
