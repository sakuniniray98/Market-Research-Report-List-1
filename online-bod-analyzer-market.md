<p><strong>The Online BOD Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Manometric Methods</li><li>Colorimetric Methods</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Online BOD Analyzer market is segmented into Manometric and Colorimetric methods for measuring biochemical oxygen demand. Manometric methods utilize pressure changes in a closed system to assess the oxygen consumption by microorganisms, providing real-time data on water quality. In contrast, Colorimetric methods rely on chemical reactions that produce a color change proportional to the BOD level, allowing for accurate and simple visual assessments. Both methods are essential for monitoring environmental compliance and ensuring water safety in various industries.</p></p>
