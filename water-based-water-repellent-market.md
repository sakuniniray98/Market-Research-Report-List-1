<p><strong>The Water Based Water Repellent Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Combining Type</li><li>Non-combining Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In the water-based water repellent market, products are classified into two types: combining and non-combining. Combining type repellents form a bond with the substrate, effectively enhancing durability and water resistance. These are often used in applications where long-lasting protection is essential. Non-combining type repellents, on the other hand, create a temporary barrier against moisture without chemically bonding to the surface, making them suitable for applications requiring easier reapplication or maintenance. Each type caters to specific performance needs and usage scenarios.</p></p>
