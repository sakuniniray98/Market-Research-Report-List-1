<p><ul><li>Stationary X-rays</li><li>Portable X-rays</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The diagnostic X-ray imaging market comprises two primary types: stationary and portable X-rays. Stationary X-rays are fixed systems typically found in hospitals and clinics, providing high-resolution images suitable for comprehensive diagnostics. They excel in areas like radiology and orthopedics. In contrast, portable X-ray units are compact, mobile devices that allow for imaging at various locations, such as emergency rooms or patients' homes. This flexibility enhances accessibility and speed in patient care, making them vital in urgent medical situations.</p></p>
