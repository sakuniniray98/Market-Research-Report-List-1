<p><ul><li>Air Cycle Refrigeration Technology</li><li>Vapor Cycle Refrigeration Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal management systems market comprises two main types: air cycle refrigeration technology and vapor cycle refrigeration technology. Air cycle refrigeration utilizes air as the working fluid, offering advantages in weight and simplicity, making it suitable for aerospace applications. In contrast, vapor cycle refrigeration relies on refrigerants undergoing phase changes to absorb and release heat, providing efficient cooling for a wide range of applications, including automotive and industrial sectors. Both technologies aim to optimize temperature control and enhance system performance.</p></p>
