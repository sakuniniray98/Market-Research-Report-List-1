<p><strong>The VOC Detection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PID</li><li>Metal-oxide Semiconductor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The VOC detection equipment market primarily includes two key types: Photoionization Detectors (PID) and Metal-Oxide Semiconductor (MOS) sensors. PID devices utilize ultraviolet light to ionize VOC molecules, offering high sensitivity and rapid response times, making them suitable for detecting low concentrations of gases. In contrast, MOS sensors detect VOCs by measuring changes in electrical resistance as gases interact with a heated semiconductor surface. Both technologies serve various industries, including environmental monitoring, safety, and industrial applications, enhancing air quality management.</p></p>
