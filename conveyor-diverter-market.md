<p><strong>The Conveyor Diverter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The conveyor diverter market is primarily categorized into two types: horizontal and vertical. Horizontal diverters guide products laterally across a conveyor system, ideal for sorting and directing items on a flat plane. They enhance productivity by efficiently routing materials without altering the conveyor's elevation. In contrast, vertical diverters redirect items upwards or downwards, suitable for multi-level systems where space is limited. Both types play crucial roles in optimizing material handling processes across various industries, ensuring smooth operations and efficient workflow.</p></p>
