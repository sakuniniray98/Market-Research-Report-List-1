<p><p>The micro pressure transmitter market is segmented into universal and cavityless types. Universal micro pressure transmitters are designed to accommodate a wide range of applications, offering flexibility in measurements and installation. Their adaptability makes them suitable for various industries. In contrast, cavityless micro pressure transmitters are engineered to minimize dead space, providing enhanced accuracy and faster response times. This makes them ideal for applications requiring precise pressure control, particularly in the pharmaceutical and food processing sectors.</p></p>
