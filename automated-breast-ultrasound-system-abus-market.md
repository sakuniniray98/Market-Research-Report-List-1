<p><p>The Automated Breast Ultrasound System (ABUS) is an advanced imaging technology designed to enhance breast cancer detection, particularly in women with dense breast tissue. This helps radiologists identify abnormalities that may not be visible in standard mammograms alone.</p><p>The ABUS market is witnessing significant growth, driven by increasing awareness of breast cancer and the importance of early detection.
