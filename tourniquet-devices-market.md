<p><p>The tourniquet devices market is categorized into three main types: pneumatic tourniquets, manual tourniquets, and other devices. Pneumatic tourniquets utilize air pressure for precise control and pressure application, ensuring effective blood flow restriction during surgical procedures. Manual tourniquets rely on manual force, offering simplicity and reliability in emergency situations. Other devices may include elastic bandages or improvised tourniquets, catering to various medical needs. Together, these types serve critical roles in managing trauma and surgical settings.</p></p>
