Non-rechargeable air batteries, often used in disposable applications, provide convenient energy solutions but cannot be reused. Rechargeable air batteries offer sustainability by allowing multiple charging cycles, making them suitable for consumer electronics and electric vehicles. Fuel cells, which convert chemical energy into electrical energy using oxygen, are employed in various applications, including transportation and stationary power. Each type serves distinct market needs and applications.</p></p>
