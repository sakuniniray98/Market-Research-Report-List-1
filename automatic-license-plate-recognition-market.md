<p><p>The Automatic License Plate Recognition (ALPR) market primarily segments into fixed and mobile types. Fixed ALPR systems are stationary installations, often positioned at key locations such as toll booths, parking lots, or entrances, providing continuous monitoring and real-time data capture. Both types serve critical roles in enhancing security, automating toll collection, and optimizing traffic management.</p></p>
