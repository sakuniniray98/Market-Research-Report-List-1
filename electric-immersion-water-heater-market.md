<p><p>An electric immersion water heater is a device that uses electrical resistance to heat water directly within a tank or container. This type of heater consists of a metal rod, typically made of copper or stainless steel, that is immersed in water. When electrical current passes through the rod, it generates heat, warming the water around it. These heaters are widely used in both residential and commercial settings for applications such as instant hot water supply, bathing, and cooking.</p><p>The Electric Immersion Water Heater Market is experiencing notable growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 7.9% during the forecast period.
