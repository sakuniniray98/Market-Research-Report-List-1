<p><ul><li>Electrically Fused To Form Zirconia</li><li>Chemical Synthesis of Zirconia</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fused Zirconia market is categorized into two main types: Electrically Fused Zirconia and chemically synthesized Zirconia. Electrically Fused Zirconia is produced by melting zircon sand in an electric arc furnace, resulting in high-purity and dense materials used in ceramics and refractories. In contrast, Chemical Synthesis of Zirconia involves chemical processes, such as precipitation or hydrolysis, to create zirconia with tailored properties. This method is often employed for applications requiring specific particle sizes and morphologies, enhancing versatility in various industrial applications.</p></p>
