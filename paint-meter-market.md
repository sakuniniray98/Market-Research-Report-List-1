<p><strong>The Paint Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-Ray Technology</li><li>Ultrasonic Technology</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The paint meter market is categorized primarily into X-ray technology and ultrasonic technology, along with other methods. </p><p>X-ray technology offers precise, non-destructive measurement of paint thickness by analyzing the interaction of X-rays with underlying substrates. Ultrasonic technology utilizes sound waves to measure paint thickness through reflection and transmission. Other methods may include magnetic induction or laser techniques, each providing unique benefits for specific applications. Together, these technologies cater to diverse industries, enabling accurate quality control and assessment of coatings.</p></p>
