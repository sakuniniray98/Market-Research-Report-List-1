<p><p>The Offshore Wind Turbine Installation Vessel market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Self-Propelled Jack-Up Vessel**: These vessels can maneuver without assistance and are equipped with retractable legs that lift them above water during installation, ensuring stability.</p><p>2. **Normal Jack-Up Vessel**: Similar to self-propelled types but require tugboats for positioning. They also use legs to elevate the platform.</p><p>3. **Heavy Lift Vessel**: Designed for transporting and installing large components, they utilize specialized cranes and are vital for heavy installations, offering flexibility for various offshore projects.</p></p>
