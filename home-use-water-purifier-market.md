<p><p>The home use water purifier market comprises various types, primarily including RO (Reverse Osmosis) water purifiers, UV (Ultraviolet) water purifiers, and other variants. RO purifiers use a membrane to remove contaminants and dissolved solids, making them effective for hard water. UV purifiers utilize ultraviolet light to kill bacteria and viruses, ensuring microbiological safety. Other types may include activated carbon filters or gravity-based purifiers, catering to diverse water quality needs and preferences, enhancing consumer choice in safe drinking water solutions.</p></p>
