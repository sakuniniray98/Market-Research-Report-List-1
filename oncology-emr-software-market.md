<p><p>Oncology EMR software is available in two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions are installed locally on the healthcare provider's servers, offering greater control and security, but requiring significant upfront investment and maintenance. In contrast, cloud-based systems operate on remote servers, providing flexibility, scalability, and easier access from various devices. These platforms typically involve lower initial costs and ongoing updates but may raise concerns about data security and compliance within healthcare regulations.</p></p>
