<p><p>The Disk Ripper market is categorized into three main types: Single Action, Offset Type, and Double Action. Single Action disk rippers use a single mechanism to penetrate the soil, ideal for minimal tilling. Offset Type disk rippers feature blades set at an angle, improving soil mixing and reducing draft. Double Action disk rippers, with two sets of disks, offer enhanced efficiency in breaking up compacted soil and incorporating residue, making them suitable for more intensive agricultural practices.</p></p>
