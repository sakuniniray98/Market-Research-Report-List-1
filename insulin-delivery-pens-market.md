<p><p>The insulin delivery pens market is divided into two main types: reusable and disposable insulin pens. Reusable insulin pens are designed for multiple uses, allowing patients to change the cartridge while retaining the pen body, which is cost-effective and eco-friendly. Conversely, disposable insulin pens are single-use devices that come prefilled with insulin, promoting convenience and reducing the risk of contamination. Both options cater to varying patient preferences, lifestyles, and needs in diabetes management, enhancing adherence to treatment regimens.</p></p>
