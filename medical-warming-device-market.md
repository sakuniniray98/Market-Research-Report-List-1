<p><p>The medical warming device market encompasses various heating technologies used to maintain patient body temperature during procedures. Electromagnetic heating utilizes magnetic fields to generate heat in tissues, offering efficiency and deep penetration. Infrared heating emits radiant heat that warms surfaces and is often used for localized treatment. Resistance heating generates heat through electrical resistivity in materials, providing consistent warmth. Each type of technology serves specific clinical needs, ensuring patient comfort and safety during surgical or recovery phases.</p></p>
